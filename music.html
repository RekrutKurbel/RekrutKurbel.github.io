Music

I actively made music from the end of 2017 until the beginning of 2020. You can find officially released stuff on my YouTube and SoundCloud profiles.

On this page I will show some unreleased stuff, scrapped stuff, experimental stuff or stuff I previewed somewhere which I made in this period (and after).
Files are sorted by creation date. Also this site may be updated in the future, since this is not everything I made and not published.

WARNING: I HAVE NOT MASTERED EVERY ONE OF THE FILES PROPERLY.
I RECOMMEND TURNING DOWN THE VOLUME OF THE AUDIO PLAYER SLIGHTLY BEFORE PLAYING ANY OF THESE.
